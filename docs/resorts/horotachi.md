## Terrain & Access Rules

### ‚ö†Ô∏è OFF-PISTE POLICY: FORBIDDEN (Strictly Enforced!)

**This is critical for us:**
- Off-piste skiing **NOT ALLOWED** between courses or in sidecountry
- Staff are known as **"off-piste Nazis"** (Powderhounds' words, not ours!)
- Similar to old-school Furano (pre-2010s)
- **You WILL be reprimanded** if caught in trees
- Visible areas (under chairlift, skier's right of chair) = absolute no-go zones

### The Trees Are Excellent... But

Despite the ban, the tree skiing here is legendary among powder freaks:
- Well-spaced trees with good pitch
- Deep, cold, dry snow (town averages -13¬∞C in winter)
- Gullies and creeks require navigation skills
- Some zones have easy ingress/egress, others are technical

**Our approach:**
- Respect the rules (it's their mountain)
- Stick to the **two ungroomed courses** for powder fun
- The "Powder Line" (Horotachi Course) is steep and legitimate
- If conditions warrant breaking rules, be **invisible** and accept **full self-responsibility**

## Hot Tips: Powder Strategy

### Why Powder Freaks Drive Here

1. **Extreme cold = best snow quality**
   - Horokanai winter avg: -13¬∞C
   - Dec 31, 2020: -32.6¬∞C recorded
   - Record low: -41.2¬∞C (coldest in Japan)
   - Snow is bone-dry, zero moisture

2. **Heavy snowfall**
   - ~13m annual average
   - 2015: 120cm fell in ONE DAY
   - 2018: 326cm max depth recorded
   - Special Heavy Snowfall Designated Area

3. **Low competition**
   - Local town population: 1,500 people
   - Even on powder days, rarely crowded
   - 7-8 people total on spring weekdays (per Mojane report)

### Best Strategy for Feb 11

**Timing:**
- Hit it early if fresh snow overnight
- Low elevation + southeast aspect = sun-affected by afternoon
- If warm temps, snow quality can sour quickly

**Runs to prioritize:**
- **Horotachi Course** (ungroomed powder line) ‚Äî first!
- **Shirakaba Course** (ungroomed trees) ‚Äî second priority
- **Karamatsu Course** (groomed but steep) ‚Äî warm-up/recovery

**Conditions to expect:**
- Late season (Feb 11) may be icy in AM, softening by midday
- Check operating schedule ‚Äî may only be open Fri-Sun by then
- If sun comes out, snow quality deteriorates fast (low elevation, sunny aspect)
